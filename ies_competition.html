Introduction

The objective of the IEEE-IES Student and Young Professional Competition 2021 is to promote standards developed by the Technical Committee on Industrial Informatics and sponsored by the Technical Committee on Standards, boost student branch chapter activities and attract student membership with the IEEE-IES.

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, this year the competition will be held online. This competition will focus on achieving industrial application development based on the industrial cloud platform, edge computing, and data management. Student and Young Professional IES members are encouraged to submit their own project achievements and industrial Apps.

Information and Important Dates

Eligibility

Any IEEE-IES student member, graduate member, or member is eligible for participating in the competition.

Prize Items

The prize will be given with a certificate and award money with three categories:

  • First Prize – 1500 USD + Certificate
  • Second Prize – 1000 USD + Certificate
  • Third Prize – 500 USD + Certificate

Track Setup

Track 1: Research Project Presentation

Individual participates should submit a 5-minutes recorded video for your research projects. The topic must be in the scope of the Industrial Electronics Society.

Track 2: Developing an Industrial Application

A group of members (maximum 3) develop a Preventative Maintenance App based on Data Analysis. Task 2 description and data set are sent out via emails. To get the task and dataset, please register your team by sending details to William Dai (w.dai@ieee.org). There is no limitation on programming language and result should be able to display on a computer.

Important Dates

  • September 6, 2021. Competition opens
  • October 13, 2021. Submission Due for track 1
  • October 19, 2021. Submission Due for track 2
  • October 22, 2021. Announcement of winners

Registration

Participates must register to enter the competition. Please send the following information to William Dai (w.dai@ieee.org) to register. Information required:

  • Name:
  • University/Institute/Company:
  • Email Address:
  • If participating in Track 1: Project Title
  • If participating in Track 2: Team Members and their emails. (The topic for track 2 will be sent out via Email after September 15, 2021)
  • IEEE membership number (With IES membership)

Submit your work

Please send your video(Track 1) or application(Track 2) via shared folder, link or email to William Dai (w.dai@ieee.org) by October 13, 2021 for track 1 and October 19, 2021 for track 2.

Committee

The competition is organized by TC on Industrial Informatics (TC-II), TC on Standards, TC on Industrial Agents (TC-IA), IES Chapter and Membership Committee, and the IES Student and Young Professionals Activities Committee (IES SYPAC).
